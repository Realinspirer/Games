Sling & Shoot!

A casual games, where you stretch your slingshot, and shoot! Eliminate the targets, but it's not gonna be easy. Many obstacles will be encountered! Can you buy the ULTIMATE Slingshot and AMMO? Collect coins as you play, and you will get closer to it! The ammo is waiting for you to get it!

[GAME OPTIMIZED FOR RUNNING IN LANDSCAPE MODE]
  • Casual
  • Has a shop and coin system
  • You can buy and change almost skin of almost any game feature in the shop
  • Playing with the TNT ammo is quite fun
  • Records hit streak and rewards player after every 15 successful hit
