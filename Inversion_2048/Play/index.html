Inversion 2048

A 2048 game, but with a twist. The world inverts itself. Along with the world, all the blocks invert themselves, and get a secondary number.

Got stuck? Look into the in-game shop, where you can spend your earned points! You can also buy some skins, but lookout for scams in the shop... well, it's in-game points anyway.

Sit down relax and enjoy INVERSION 2048.
  • Casual
  • In-game shop and points system
  • Two game modes - Progressive and Limitless
  • 2048 but world inverts itself
  • Block skins
