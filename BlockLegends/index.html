Match blocks & Clear!

Drag and arrange blocks in a line, either vertically or horizontally to match and clear them.

Unlock Challenges & Missions

As you level up more and more, you will unlock several challenges during your run like vines and bigger blocks. Keep a look out for the Missions tab. Complete missions and earn rewards!

Bombs & Diamonds

As you clear more and more blocks, you will find bombs and diamonds on your blocks. Bombs will destroy neighboring blocks, and diamonds can help you level up!

Fun powerups

You will find powerups at the bottom of the table. Earn these powerups by using Diamonds or completing mission. You can use these powerups to breeze through levels!
